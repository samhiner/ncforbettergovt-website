Our Issues

Our entire focus is currently on advocating for legislation in the NC General Assembly to address lame-duck power grabs. If we are successful in having an impact with this issue, then we will expand into advocating for other good-government issues. No matter what issues we expand into, our focus will always be on promoting nonpartisan solutions to problems in North Carolina's government.
